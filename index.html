This plot show how ’clique-y’ certain series are measured by the characters’ network-modularity. The ’cliqueyness’ distribution is similar for series with high or low imdb-rating, the distribution for mediocre series has smaller variance. One intuitive explaination is that if a series has unconventional value of modularity, than it will be received either better or worse, conventional levels of cliqueyness result in conventional ratings.
